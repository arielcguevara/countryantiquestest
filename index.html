Our Story

Where Every Piece
Has a Past

For over 40 years, Country Antiques & Curiosities has been the Upstate South Carolina destination for treasure hunters, collectors, and everyone who believes the best things in life have a little history to them.

Spread across more than 7,000 square feet, our space is home to a community of multiple passionate vendors, each bringing their own unique collection — from American farmhouse furniture to vintage jewelry, estate finds, folk art, and rare collectibles.

Whether you're a lifelong collector or just discovering the joy of vintage, there's always something new to uncover at Country Antiques & Curiosities.
